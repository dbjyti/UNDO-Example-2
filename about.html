Our Story

About UNDO®

Our Mission

Born from the Need for Something Better

UNDO® was developed by veteran permanent makeup and tattoo artists who saw the same story repeat itself: a client regrets their tattoo or permanent makeup, searches for removal options, and is told the only choice is painful, expensive laser treatment — with no guarantee of success.

We knew there had to be a better way. Years of research, testing, and refinement later, UNDO® was born: a patent-pending, non-laser pigment removal system built from the ground up to be safe, effective, and accessible.

"Every client deserves a safe path forward — regardless of their skin tone, the ink color, or the treatment area."

Today, UNDO® is used by certified professionals across the USA and Canada to safely remove permanent makeup, scalp micropigmentation, and small to medium body tattoos — with no laser, no burning, and no scarring.

Safety First

Formulated with natural ingredients safe for all skin types, including delicate areas like the eyes.

Efficacy

Designed to deliver visible results with the minimum number of sessions possible.

Accessibility

Works on all skin tones and ink colors — a true universal removal solution.

Education

Our Journey

The UNDO® Story

2016

The Problem Recognized

Veteran permanent makeup artists begin documenting client frustration with laser removal — burns, scarring, and incomplete removal — and start researching alternatives.

2018

Formula Development Begins

A team of artists and cosmetic chemists collaborate on a natural, non-thermal approach to pigment removal. Early prototypes are tested with promising results.

2019

Clinical Testing & Refinement

UNDO® undergoes rigorous testing across diverse skin types and ink colors. The multi-step system is refined to minimize sessions and maximize safety.

UNDO® officially launches with its patent-pending formula. The first certified technicians complete in-person training in Florida.

Expansion to Canada

Growing demand leads to UNDO® expanding to Canada, with a dedicated distribution network and training program established for Canadian professionals.

Online Training & Global Reach

UNDO® Masterclass online training launches, bringing professional certification to practitioners worldwide without the need for travel.

The Formula

What's Inside UNDO®

Active Agent

Lactic Acid Complex

A natural alpha-hydroxy acid that gently exfoliates and helps draw pigment to the surface of the skin for removal.

Soothing

Aloe Vera Extract

Provides anti-inflammatory support, reducing redness and discomfort during and after treatment.

Healing

Vitamin C Ester

Promotes collagen production and helps even skin tone during the healing process between sessions.

Brightening

Kojic Acid

A naturally derived brightening agent that helps break down residual pigment and promotes uniform skin tone.

Hydrating

Hyaluronic Acid

Maintains skin hydration throughout treatment, supporting the skin barrier and reducing downtime.

Antimicrobial

Tea Tree Oil

Natural antimicrobial properties help protect the treated area during the healing process.

Important Information

Who Should Avoid UNDO®

UNDO® is safe for most adults. However, certain conditions require avoiding treatment. Your certified technician will review your health history before any procedure.

⚠

Pregnancy & Nursing

UNDO® should not be used on anyone who is pregnant or currently nursing.

⚠

Diabetes

Diabetic clients should not receive UNDO® treatment due to impaired skin healing.

⚠

Blood Thinners

Clients taking blood thinning medications should avoid treatment.

⚠

Chemotherapy

Anyone currently undergoing chemotherapy or radiation therapy is not a candidate.

⚠

Active Skin Irritations

Rashes, sunburn, active acne, or any skin irritation near the treatment area must be resolved first.

⚠

Recent Botox

Clients who have had Botox within the past two weeks near the treatment area should wait before proceeding.
