01General Questions
What is UNDO® Non-Laser Tattoo Removal?+
UNDO® is a patent-pending, multi-step non-laser pigment removal system formulated with natural ingredients. It safely removes unwanted permanent makeup, scalp micropigmentation, and small to medium body tattoos — without the burns, scarring, or downtime associated with laser treatment.
How is UNDO® different from laser removal?+
Laser removal uses concentrated heat energy to shatter ink particles, which can cause burns, scarring, and hypopigmentation — especially on darker skin tones. UNDO® uses a natural chemical process to draw pigment out of the skin, avoiding heat damage entirely. It also works safely in delicate areas like around the eyes where laser is too risky.
Who developed UNDO®?+
UNDO® was developed by veteran permanent makeup and tattoo artists who saw a need for a safer, more effective alternative to laser removal. The formula was refined over several years of testing with clients across diverse skin types and ink colors.
Is UNDO® available in my country?+
UNDO® is currently available across the USA and Canada through our certified technician network. We are actively expanding globally — contact us to inquire about availability in your region or to become a distributor.
02Treatment & Safety
Is UNDO® safe for all skin types?+
Yes. UNDO® is formulated to work safely across all skin types and tones. Unlike laser, it does not pose the same risk of hypopigmentation on deeper skin tones. It is also safe for use in delicate areas such as around the eyes.
Does UNDO® work on all ink colors?+
Yes. UNDO® is effective on a wide range of ink and pigment colors. During your consultation, your certified technician will assess your specific ink type and color to set appropriate expectations for your treatment plan.
Is the treatment painful?+
Most clients describe UNDO® treatment as mildly uncomfortable — similar to getting the original tattoo. A topical numbing cream is applied before treatment to maximize your comfort. There is typically some redness and mild swelling in the days following treatment, which resolves with proper aftercare.
Who should NOT receive UNDO® treatment?+
UNDO® is not suitable for individuals who are pregnant or nursing, diabetic, taking blood thinners or prescription acne medication (such as Accutane), undergoing chemotherapy, have major heart problems, or have active skin irritations near the treatment area. A full health consultation is conducted before any treatment.
What is the downtime after treatment?+
Most clients experience 7–14 days of healing after each session, during which the treated area will scab and heal. Clients should avoid sun exposure, swimming, and any skincare products on the treated area during healing. Your technician will provide detailed aftercare instructions.
03Results & Expectations
How many sessions will I need?+
The number of sessions varies depending on the type of ink, the depth of application, the treatment area, and individual healing. Most clients see significant lightening in 2–4 sessions, with full removal typically achieved in 4–8 sessions. Permanent makeup often takes fewer sessions than body tattoos due to shallower ink placement.
How far apart are sessions?+
Sessions are typically spaced 6–8 weeks apart to allow the skin to fully heal between treatments. Rushing sessions can compromise results and increase the risk of complications.
Will UNDO® completely remove my tattoo?+
In most cases, yes — complete removal is achievable. However, results vary based on ink type, color, depth, and individual skin factors. Some clients achieve complete removal while others achieve significant lightening that allows for coverup work. Your technician will give you realistic expectations during your consultation.
Will there be scarring?+
When performed by a certified UNDO® technician and proper aftercare is followed, scarring is extremely rare. UNDO® is specifically designed to minimize tissue trauma. Following your aftercare instructions carefully is critical to achieving the best outcome.
04Training & Certification
Who can train to become a certified UNDO® technician?+
UNDO® training is open to licensed permanent makeup artists, tattoo artists, estheticians, and medical professionals. No prior removal experience is necessary. All students must hold an active professional license in their applicable field.
What training formats are available?+
We offer a 2-day in-person Masterclass at our Boca Raton, Florida location, and a self-paced Online Masterclass with lifetime access. Both programs lead to official UNDO® certification. See our Training page for full details.
Is the online certification as comprehensive as in-person?+
The online curriculum covers the same theoretical content as the in-person program. The key difference is that in-person training includes live model practice sessions and a starter kit. Many students choose the online format first and attend an in-person workshop later for hands-on practice.
Do I need to renew my certification?+
UNDO® certification does not expire, but we recommend attending refresher workshops when available as the formula and protocols are periodically updated. All certified technicians receive notifications about updates through our community platform.
05Pricing & Booking
How much does a UNDO® treatment session cost?+
Session pricing varies by technician and location. Most UNDO® practitioners charge between $150–$400 per session depending on the size of the treatment area and region. Use our Find a Technician tool to connect with a certified practitioner near you and ask about their pricing.
Is there a consultation fee?+
Consultation policies vary by technician. Some offer free initial consultations while others charge a small fee that is often credited toward your first treatment. Contact your chosen technician directly for their specific policy.
How do I book a training spot?+
You can enroll in online training directly through our website. For in-person training, please visit our Contact page to inquire about upcoming dates and availability. In-person courses have limited seats and fill quickly.
Are group discounts available for training?+
Yes — studios enrolling 3 or more technicians are eligible for group discounts. Contact us directly for a custom group enrollment quote.
