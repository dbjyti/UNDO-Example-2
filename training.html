Do I need prior experience to enroll?+
UNDO® training is open to licensed professionals including permanent makeup artists, tattoo artists, estheticians, and medical professionals. No prior removal experience is required.
How long does certification take?+
In-person training is a 2-day intensive. The online masterclass is self-paced — most students complete it within 2–4 weeks while working their existing schedule.
Is the certification recognized internationally?+
UNDO® certification is currently recognized across the USA and Canada. We are actively working to expand our recognition network globally.
Can I purchase UNDO® products after training?+
Yes — certified UNDO® technicians have access to our professional product store at certified practitioner pricing.
Are there group or studio discounts?+
Yes, we offer group enrollment discounts for studios enrolling 3 or more technicians. Contact us for a custom quote.
