What Is UNDO?

A Safer Path to Clear Skin

UNDO® was developed and tested by veteran permanent makeup and tattoo artists who recognized the need for a safer removal option for their clients. Where lasers can cause burns, scarring, and discomfort, UNDO® offers a gentle, multi-step alternative.

Formulated with premium natural ingredients, UNDO® minimizes the number of sessions needed and works on all skin types — even in delicate areas like the eyes.

The Process

How UNDO® Works

01
Consultation

Your certified UNDO® technician evaluates your skin type, ink color, and treatment area to create a personalized removal plan.

02
Preparation

The skin is gently cleaned and prepped. A topical numbing agent may be applied to maximize your comfort during the procedure.

03
Application

The UNDO® solution is applied using a professional device, working to draw pigment from the skin with precision.

04
Aftercare

Detailed aftercare instructions are provided. Most clients see visible lightening after the first session with full removal typically in 2–6 sessions.
